About Me

I'm a passionate Telecom Engineer, Full-Stack Developer, and Graphic Designer with a Bachelor's degree in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering from Dar es Salaam Institute of Technology (DIT).

With over 5 years of experience in the tech industry, I specialize in creating innovative solutions that bridge the gap between telecommunications and software development. My expertise spans from network infrastructure design to full-stack web and mobile application development.

I'm an aspiring Automation Engineer. Passionate about Systems Integration involving Web Development, SCADA/HMI/PLC Application Development. I possess programing knowledge of major brands including Siemens, Rockwell Automation and Panasonic. I also leverage on the use of HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Node-Red) and Python.

As the CEO and Co-founder of Aset Co. Ltd, I lead a team dedicated to delivering high-quality services that make a difference. We focus on providing solutions that stand out in quality, timeliness, and technological advancement.
